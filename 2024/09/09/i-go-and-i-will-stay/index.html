i go and i will stay

2024-09-09
|
  • 随笔
|
访问

There are two things that we should never have to chase: true friends and true love.

​ People make time for who they want to make time for. When someone tells you that they are too busy, believe them. Don't try to force them to make time for you, if they want to, they will.

​ It hurts, but you can't force someone to have feelings for you.

​ You should not have to beg someone to love you.

​ You should not have to beg someone to care.

​ You should not have to beg someone to try.

​ You should not have to beg someone to talk to you.

​ And you should not have to beg someone to put you first.

​ Don't let people be a priority in your life when they treat you like an option.

​ Don't beg someone to stay when they all want to do is to leave.

​ Instead, hold open the door for them.
